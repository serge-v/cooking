Russian Kvass

Ingredients for 1.8 liters ( 2 qt. ) of Kvass:
  • Black bread (2 slices )
  • Dry cooking yeast ( 5 g or tablespoon )
  • Sugar (140 g or 9 tablespoons )
Steps to execute:
  • Toast bread in oven or grill for 20-30 min. Not burn but near
  • Put bread into glass bottle and pour a boiled water.
  • Wait for 2 hours
